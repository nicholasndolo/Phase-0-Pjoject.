Depression among the young generation.

Cases of depression among young people especially in the developing counties is on the rise.A research conducted on some university students in Africa has shown that men are the main victims. The research goes ahaed to highlight the possible cause of cause of depression among the young in developing countries.

Major Causes

  • Unemployment
  • Substance abuse
  • Teenage pregnancies
  • Death/Loss of a supportive family member
  • Sexual abuse

This research was conducted on persons aged between 15 -25 years of age.
